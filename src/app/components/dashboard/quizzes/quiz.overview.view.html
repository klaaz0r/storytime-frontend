15 men on a dead man’s chest

Fifteen men on the dead man’s chest – Yo-ho-ho and a bottle of rum!

Drink and the devil had done

For the rest – Yo-ho-ho and a bottle of rum! The mate was fixed by the bos’n's pike

The bos’n brained

With a marlinspike and Cookey’s throat was marked belike. It had been gripped

By fingers ten

And there they lay All good dead men, like break-o’-day in a boozing-ken

Yo-ho and a bottle of rum!
